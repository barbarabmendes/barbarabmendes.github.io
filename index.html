Research

For nearly five years, I have been researching and engineering intrinsically bioactive systems as mimics of the native extracellular matrix that find multiple research and clinical applications in regenerative medicine and tissue engineering field.

Education

Since then she is conducting her PhD research at the 3B’s Research Group, I3Bs Institute of the University of Minho, under the supervision of Prof. Manuela Gomes and Dr. Rui Domingues, and in collaboration with the University of Pennsylvania (USA) under the supervision of Professor Jason Burdick.
